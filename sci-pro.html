Nurturing scientific curiosity and excellence since 1985.

YTROZ Royal College's Science Programs provide students with a strong foundation in scientific principles while encouraging critical thinking and hands-on experimentation. Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards and prepare students for higher education and careers in various scientific fields.

World-Class Facilities

State-of-the-art resources for scientific exploration

Advanced Chemistry Lab

Fully equipped with modern instrumentation including GC-MS, HPLC, and spectrophotometers.

Physics Research Center

Featuring quantum computing equipment, laser optics, and advanced measurement systems.

Biological Sciences Complex

Includes tissue culture facilities, DNA sequencing equipment, and ecological research stations.

Computational Science Lab

High-performance computing clusters for data analysis and scientific modeling.

Botanical Research Greenhouse

Climate-controlled environments for plant science research.

Astronomical Observatory

16-inch telescope with spectroscopy capabilities for astrophysics research.

Admissions Process

Start your scientific journey at YTROZ Royal College

1
Application Submission

Complete our online application form with required documents by March 31.

2
Entrance Examination

Take our science aptitude test (April 15) covering math and scientific reasoning.

3
Interview

Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with faculty members.

4
Acceptance & Enrollment

Receive your acceptance letter and complete enrollment by June 1.
